Our History & Mission

Precision Healthcare was founded in 1990 by Dr. Christopher Lee with the vision of providing top-tier medical care that blends cutting-edge technology with personalized, patient-centered services. What started as a small clinic quickly gained a reputation for its high-quality care and commitment to community health.

As the demand for specialized medical services grew, Precision Healthcare expanded, and in 2005, it opened its flagship hospital, offering a full range of medical services from diagnostics to rehabilitation.

In response to the growing needs of the community, two more branches were established. In 2012, Precision Surgical Center was launched, specializing in complex surgeries with state-of-the-art operating rooms and a highly skilled surgical team.

In 2016, Precision Children’s Hospital opened its doors, providing specialized care for pediatric patients in a welcoming and family-friendly environment.

Today, Precision Healthcare is known for its unwavering commitment to excellence and its holistic approach to healing across its three branches.
