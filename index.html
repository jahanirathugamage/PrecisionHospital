Help & FAQ.

FAQ.

Have questions or need assistance?
Explore our frequently asked questions to find the answers you're looking for or get in touch with our support team.

  1. What services does Precision Hospital offer?
    Precision Hospital provides primary care, specialized treatments, surgery, diagnostics, rehab, and emergency care across various departments.

  2. How can I schedule an appointment?
    Schedule by calling us, booking online, or visiting our outpatient department. Our staff will help find the right time and specialist.

  3. Does Precision Hospital accept my insurance?
    We accept most major insurance plans. Contact our billing department or check our website to confirm your plan is accepted.

  4. What should I bring to my first appointment?
    Bring your ID, insurance card, medication list, relevant medical records, and any concerns to discuss with the doctor.

  5. Are emergency services available 24/7?
    Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7 for urgent care needs, from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions.

  6. How do I obtain a copy of my medical records?
    Request records by visiting or calling our records office, or filling out a form online. Processing may take a few days.
